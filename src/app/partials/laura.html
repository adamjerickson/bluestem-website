Executive Director

Laura Erickson

Laura Erickson is the Executive Director of Bluestem. Coming from a Montessori family herself, she is passionate about the benefits of a Montessori education.

more

Her own Montessori education developed a life-long love of learning that has led her to a BA from Hastings College, a MA in Medieval History from the University of Washington, and most of the way through a PhD (oh that pesky dissertation!). In 2008 she became a Fulbright Scholar, and took her husband and baby daughter to study medieval manuscripts in Germany. She has taught hundreds of college students as a teaching assistant and then as an instructor, and was recognized by her department for her excellence in teaching.
